Location Information and Registration

Registration

The registration system will be opened closer to the event. The registration fees are outlined below; prices are in Australian dollar and include 1 banquet dinner.


Regular Early Bird $600.00 AUD
Regular Standard $710.00 AUD
Student Early Bird $355.00 AUD
Student Standard $470.00 AUD

Early bird registration closes on 11 September 2015. Student travel scholarships are also available.


Location Information

AIRS 2015 is being held at the Queensland University of Technolgy in Brisbane, Australia. More information about how to get there will be posted closer to the conference date.
